About Us

The haunted minds behind Ghost's Creations

Ghost's Creations is an independent Roblox game development studio obsessed with crafting dark, immersive, and atmospheric experiences. We believe great games should make you feel something — fear, wonder, curiosity. We push the boundaries of what's possible on the Roblox platform and deliver worlds you won't forget.
